--- Prompt ---
Identify the parts of speech in the sentence: Quickly, the agile cat climbed the tall tree.
--- Answer ---
Identify the parts of speech in the sentence: Quickly, the agile cat climbed the tall tree.

Here's the breakdown:

* **Quickly:** Adverb (describes how the cat climbed)
* **the:** Definite article (specifies a particular cat)
* **agile:** Adjective (describes the cat)
* **cat:** Noun (the subject of the sentence)
