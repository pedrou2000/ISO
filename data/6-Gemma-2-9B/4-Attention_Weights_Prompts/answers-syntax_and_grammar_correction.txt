Correct the error: He go to school every day.
--- Answer ---
Correct the error: He go to school every day.


The correct sentence is: **He goes to school every day.**


**Explanation:**

The verb "to go" needs to agree with the subject "He".  Since "He" is singular, the present tense form of the verb is "goes".
